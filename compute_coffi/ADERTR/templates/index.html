TR Time Reconstruction β-Matrix Generator for Arbitrary History Points

Description: Given $h_0$ (the next time step) and the historical intervals $h_1,\dots,h_N$, construct nodes $t_0=0, t_k=-\sum_{i=1}^k h_i$, build the Lagrange basis $L_i(t)$, and compute $$ B_{ij}=\sum_{l=1}^{\ell_{\max}}\int_0^{h_0} h_0^{2l-1}\,L_i^{(l)}(t)\,L_j^{(l)}(t)\,dt. $$ Also compute the derivative mapping matrix $D$, which satisfies $v^{(m)}(t_n)=\sum_k D_{m,k} v(t_{n-k})$.

Example: h0 or 2.0

Tips

  • Supports both symbolic (e.g. h0, h1, h2) and numeric (e.g. 0.5, 0.2) inputs.
  • \(\ell_{\max}\) is usually equal to the number of history points \(N\). To reproduce your original code’s \(l=1,2\), set \(\ell_{\max}=2\).
  • For large \(N\), symbolic integration may be slow — test with numeric values first.
